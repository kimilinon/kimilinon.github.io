About the film



Welcome to the official page of the highly anticipated film adaptation of "Project Hail Mary," the gripping science fiction novel by acclaimed author Andy Weir. Prepare to embark on an awe-inspiring journey through the vast expanse of space, guided by the visionary minds of Phil Lord and Christopher Miller, the creative duo behind unforgettable cinematic experiences.

"Project Hail Mary" takes us on an extraordinary adventure, intertwining thrilling scientific discovery with the resilience of the human spirit. Adapted from Andy Weir's captivating bestseller, the movie brings to life a story that will captivate audiences across the globe.

Set against the backdrop of an impending global catastrophe, the film follows the remarkable journey of Ryland Grace, a brilliant scientist facing the monumental task of saving humanity. Stranded aboard a spacecraft, with only fragments of his memory intact, Grace must unravel the mystery behind his own existence and find a solution to the crisis that threatens life on Earth.

Guided by the deft hands of Phil Lord and Christopher Miller, renowned for their ingenious storytelling and unique ability to blend humor and heart, "Project Hail Mary" promises a cinematic experience like no other. The directors' uncanny talent for infusing every frame with a perfect balance of emotion, wit, and visual splendor will transport audiences to the furthest reaches of the universe.

Brought to life by a stellar ensemble cast, the characters of "Project Hail Mary" will leave an indelible mark on your imagination. Witness their determination, bravery, and vulnerability as they grapple with the impossible, their performances resonating long after the credits roll.

As the cosmic odyssey unfolds, prepare to be mesmerized by awe-inspiring visuals, meticulously crafted special effects, and a meticulously constructed world that will immerse you in the marvels and dangers of space exploration. The movie will push the boundaries of storytelling, creating an unforgettable tapestry of scientific wonder, human ingenuity, and the bonds that connect us all.

We invite you to join us on this unparalleled cinematic voyage, as "Project Hail Mary" propels us toward the unknown, igniting our collective imagination and reminding us of the strength and beauty of the human spirit. Get ready to be astounded, thrilled, and moved beyond measure as we embark on a mission that will shape the destiny of humanity itself.

Discover the wonders of the universe and witness the triumph of the human spirit in "Project Hail Mary," coming soon to theaters near you.
